About the Festival

A Night Where Dreams Take Flight.

As darkness falls, thousands of glowing lanterns rise, carrying wishes, dreams, and love into the sky. Night of Light is a magical celebration of hope and togetherness, inspired by the timeless tradition of floating lanterns. Personalize your lantern, release it into the night, and watch as the sky fills with golden light. Enjoy live music, performances, food, and art in an atmosphere of wonder and joy. Join us for an unforgettable evening where the night glows with dreams and endless possibilities.

Event Schedule

Lantern Lift-Off: The Grand Lantern Release

The highlight of the Night of Light Festival, the Grand Lantern Release is an awe-inspiring spectacle. Attendees are invited to personalize their lanterns with wishes, dreams, and messages of hope before sending them into the night sky. As thousands of lanterns float upwards, the sky transforms into a glowing sea of light, creating an unforgettable moment of magic and togetherness.

Crafting the Glow: Lantern-Making Workshop

Unleash your creativity at the Lantern-Making Workshop! In this hands-on activity, festival-goers can design and craft their very own lanterns using a variety of materials, from colorful paper to decorative embellishments. Local artisans will guide you through the process, sharing tips on calligraphy, painting, and customizing your lantern with personal messages or symbols. Once finished, you can release your creation into the sky during the Grand Lantern Release!
